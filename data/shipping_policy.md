# Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 1 to 3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

We offer the following shipping methods:
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days ($5.99)
- Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($15.00)
- Overnight Shipping: 1 business day ($25.00)

Free standard shipping is available for all domestic orders over $75.00.

International shipping is available to select countries. Delivery times for international orders vary between 10 and 21 business days depending on the destination and customs processing. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes incurred.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number. Tracking information is usually updated within 24 hours of shipment.

We do not ship to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO addresses at this time.
